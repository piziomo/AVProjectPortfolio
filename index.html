About Me.

An experienced CEDIA certified Electronic Systems Designer with a passion for IoT, AV Systems Design/integration. With 3+ years of hands-on experience, I specialize in designing and implementing high-performance AV, security, and automation systems for residential and commercial projects. My expertise in Crestron, Lutron, Sonos, Hikvision, and Control4, Wisdom Audio, etc. allow me to create efficient, user-friendly, and high-tech environments tailored to client needs. I take pride in delivering seamless, intuitive, and functional solutions that enhance user experience and efficiency. I am eager to bring my technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities to a dynamic team.
